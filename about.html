OUR CORPORATE PROFILE

We are a prominent engineering and consulting conglomerate specializing in high-performance Processing and Packaging solutions. Established around 2008 by Mr. Dhyan Gandhi, the company has grown from a local manufacturer into a premier consultant and technology provider for the pharmaceutical, food, and chemical industries.

We provide Turnkey Solutions for setting up entire manufacturing plants, primarily for the food, pharma, Chemical , Agro , Spices , Paints , cosmetic etc industries.
