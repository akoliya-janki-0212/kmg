Managing Director

Dhyan Shah is the Founder and Managing Director of KMG Industries, a leading engineering and consulting firm based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Under his leadership since 2008, the company has grown into a global provider of turnkey plant solutions and industrial consulting.

Professional Roles & Expertise
Dhyan Shah operates multiple business entities and roles within the industrial sector:

Industrial Consultant

He provides expert consulting for the food and pharmaceutical industries, specializing in production planning, control, and process optimization.

Founder of KMG Industries

He established KMG Industries, which offers complete, sustainable, and high-output industrial solutions for startups and large corporates alike.

Lead at KMG Pharma

He is also associated with KMG Pharma and Packaging Industries, which deals in organic spices, chocolates, and automatic packaging plants.
