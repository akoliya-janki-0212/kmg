A flow pack machine (also known as a flow wrapping machine) is used to wrap solid products in a continuous film of plastic or packaging material. It is widely used for packing items like biscuits, chocolates, bakery products, soaps, and medical supplies.

This machine creates a tight, sealed package that protects the product from dust, moisture, and damage. It is known for its fast operation, neat appearance, and strong sealing, making it suitable for high-speed industrial packaging.
