An impact pulverizer is a high-speed industrial grinding machine that uses rotating hammers to reduce materials like spices, chemicals, minerals, and grains into fine powders, typically ranging from 100 to 300 mesh. Featuring a rotor, hammers, and a whizzer classifier, it offers adjustable fineness and is suitable for both medium-hard and soft materials in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.
