A powder filling machine is an industrial machine used to accurately fill powder products into containers like bottles, pouches, jars, or bags. These machines are widely used in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and cosmetics.

⚙️ How It Works
The machine stores powder in a hopper (container at the top).
It uses a mechanism (like an auger screw or gravity system) to measure the required quantity.
The powder is then dispensed into containers.
The process can be manual, semi-automatic, or fully automatic.
