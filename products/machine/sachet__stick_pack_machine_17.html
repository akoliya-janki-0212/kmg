A sachet & stick pack machine is a packaging machine used to fill and seal small quantities of products into compact packets like sachets or stick-shaped packs. These machines are commonly used for products such as sugar, coffee, shampoo, ketchup, powders, and liquids.

They are designed to produce uniform, lightweight, and easy-to-carry packaging, making them ideal for single-use or trial-size products. The machine ensures accurate filling, proper sealing, and high-speed production, which is essential for mass production in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.
