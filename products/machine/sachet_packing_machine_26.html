Sachet Packing Machine

Sub-CategorySpices Processing
ModelKMG
Sachet Packing Machine is used for packing small quantities of powders, liquids, and granules in compact sachets. It performs forming, filling, and sealing in a single automated cycle, ensuring efficiency and accuracy. This type of packaging is commonly used in food, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical industries where portion control and convenience are important. Compact design saves space while delivering high output. Machine also ensures uniform sealing quality for better product safety and shelf life.
